Account Deletion Instructions

If you wish to permanently delete your Fit account and all associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the Fit app on your device.
  2. Tap on the Settings icon in the top bar.
  3. Scroll down and select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm your choice by tapping Delete again. This action cannot be undone.
