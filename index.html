Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Fit app. We are committed to protecting your data and explaining how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

  • Information you provide voluntarily:
    Your email and password when registering, as well as your name and profile picture retrieved from your account.
  • Automatically collected data:
    Device type, operating system, and app usage logs (which screens and features you use).

2. How We Use Data

  • Organize workout programs and save your progress in Firebase.
  • Display relevant ads via AdMob.
  • Manage paid subscriptions through Google Play Billing.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share your data with any external parties except our technical service providers, who process data on our behalf to operate the app, including:

  • Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Analytics)
  • Google Play (Billing)
  • AdMob (Ads display)

4. Security

  • We use HTTPS to encrypt all data in transit.
  • We store data in secure Firebase databases.

5. User Rights

You have full control over your data. If you wish to delete your account and all associated data, you can do so directly through the app in the Settings > Delete Account section. This action is irreversible and will remove your data from our systems.
